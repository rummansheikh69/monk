Gameplay 

Game Initiation and Setup
When a player accesses $MONKY, the experience begins by connecting their digital wallet, especially if they own an $MONKY from the project. Upon entering the platform, the player clicks on Create Game, which triggers the AI to generate a completely new investigation. The AI randomly selects a set of $MONKY cards from the collection and uses them as building blocks to craft an intricate crime story. This generated narrative includes elements such as the crime itself, the crime scene, the perpetrator, witnesses, evidence, locations, and bystanders. Some cards chosen by the AI are deliberately included to add complexity and serve as distractions, creating red herrings that do not necessarily contribute to solving the case.

Dynamic World and Map
$MONKY features a fixed map with predetermined locations. However, the characters and objects that populate these areas change with each AI-generated story, ensuring a unique experience in every playthrough. Once the AI constructs the initial narrative, the investigation begins.

Exploration and Evidence Collection
Players are presented with the details of the crime they must solve. They navigate through the map, exploring various locations, searching for evidence, and interacting with non-player characters (NPCs). Each time the player encounters a character or an object of interest, a corresponding card is added to their collection. This collection represents all the clues, suspects, evidence, and related items encountered during the investigation.

Transition to the Crime Board
At a pivotal moment, players move from exploration to analysis by entering the Crime Board phase. Here, they open their card pack and transfer all collected cards onto the Crime Board—a flexible, open workspace where the investigator arranges and organizes their clues. As the detective, the player has complete freedom to arrange the cards in any way that makes sense to them. The board comes with minimal rules, emphasizing player autonomy in how they approach the investigation.

Connecting Clues on the Crime Board
Once the cards are laid out, the player begins drawing lines between cards to hypothesize connections. For example, if the player noticed a scenario where they met a model and later encountered a Volkswagen Beetle, they might connect those two cards, reasoning that the car belongs to the model. The system already knows which connections are correct, as the AI established the underlying relationships when constructing the story.

  • Correct Connections: When a connection is valid, the line turns green, signaling a correct deduction. This positive feedback propels the investigation forward, unlocking additional parts of the story, new clues, or areas on the map.
  • Incorrect Connections: If the player draws a line between two unrelated cards, the line turns red. Such mistakes reflect a misstep in the investigation, costing the detective one coffee cup, which symbolizes lost time and energy, and halting the progress temporarily.

The Role of Coffee Cups
The game is fundamentally built around minimizing mistakes. Each error costs the player a coffee cup, which serves as a measure of the detectives energy. Free-to-play users receive a limited number of coffee cups per day (typically 3-5), representing how many mistakes they can afford before they must pause their investigation. Once their daily allotment is exhausted, players must wait until the cups recharge on the next day to continue making further mistakes. There are options to purchase additional coffee cups through one-time cryptocurrency payments. Importantly, $MONKY holders enjoy unlimited coffee cups, ensuring they never run out of energy for investigation—this grants them a smoother, uninterrupted gameplay experience.

Unveiling the Mystery
As the player continues to connect relevant cards correctly, the investigation deepens. The dynamic narrative unfolds progressively, revealing more details about the crime. Gradually, players piece together who committed the crime, which weapon was used, and what objects or clues were instrumental in carrying out the offense. The AI occasionally designs cases with multiple culprits, enhancing complexity and replayability. Each correct deduction brings the player closer to unraveling the mystery, providing a sense of accomplishment and clarity as the truth behind the crime is progressively unveiled.
